GUIWindows.cpp - 09-05-2026
==============================
Bug fix: experimental button callback used Sleep(10) loop waiting for FadeToBlack
to complete, but this callback runs on the render thread. Sleeping blocked RenderFrame,
so the fade never advanced (IsFadeActive() stayed true for the full 3-second timeout).
This also caused the recorder to be starved of video frames for 3 seconds, increasing
the window for the concurrent WriteSample race with AudioCaptureThread.

Fixed by removing the Sleep/spin-wait. The FadeToBlack is started and the scene
transition happens immediately; the fade completes visually on its own as the render
loop resumes after the callback returns.
