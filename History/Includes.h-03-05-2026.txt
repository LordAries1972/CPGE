========================
Added: LookupAspectRatio(int w, int h) inline function.

Returns the canonical aspect ratio (as float) for a standard resolution by
matching against a built-in table of known display modes:
  16:9   — 1280x720 through 7680x4320
  16:10  — 1280x800 through 2560x1600
  21:9   — 2560x1080, 3440x1440, 5120x2160
  32:9   — 3840x1080, 5120x1440
  4:3    — 640x480 through 2048x1536
  3:2    — 480x320, 1152x768, 1440x960
  5:4    — 1280x1024, 2560x2048
  17:9   — 2048x1080 (DCI 2K), 4096x2160 (DCI 4K)

Falls back to (float)w/h for non-standard resolutions.
Used by DX11Renderer::Resize() and GUIConfigWindow resolution callback.
