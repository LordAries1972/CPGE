WarpDotTunnel smooth camera transition — 16/05/2026 (sixth pass)

Init3DWarpDOTTunnel:
- data.smoothLookTarget seeded to XMFLOAT3(x, y, data.farZ) so the first frame the camera
  already aims straight down the tunnel.

UpdateWarpDotTunnel — camera block:
- Previous: SetTarget(lookRing.cx, lookRing.cy, lookRing.zPos) — hard snap every frame.
- New: exponential ease toward the look ring position:
    alpha = 1.0 - exp(-kCameraSmooth * dt)   // framerate-independent
    smoothLookTarget.xyz += (lookRing.xyz - smoothLookTarget.xyz) * alpha
    SetTarget(smoothLookTarget)
- Rate kCameraSmooth = 3.0 → ~95% of gap closed in ~1 second at any frame rate.
Same change applied to VULKAN_FXManager.cpp.
