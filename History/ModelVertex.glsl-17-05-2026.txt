  location 1: aNormal   (vec3)
  location 2: aTexCoord (vec2)
  location 3: aTangent  (vec3)
Outputs: vWorldPosition, vNormal, vTexCoord, vViewDirection, vTangent, vBitangent

HLSL->GLSL key changes:
  mul(v,m) -> m*v (column-major); float4x4 -> mat4; SV_POSITION -> gl_Position
