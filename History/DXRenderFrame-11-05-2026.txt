Changes Made (2026-05-11):
--------------------------
Integrated ScriptManager per-frame Update() call and extern declaration into
DXRenderFrame.cpp with full #ifdef __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__ guards.

1. After extern bFullScreenTransition declaration (before #pragma warning(push)):
   - Added:
       #ifdef __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__
       #include "ScriptManager.h"
       extern ScriptManager scriptManager;
       #endif
   - Including ScriptManager.h here is safe; all its dependencies are already
     pulled in via Includes.h and the other includes above. #pragma once prevents
     duplicate processing.

2. After deltaTime = std::clamp(...) (before lastFrameTime = now):
   - Added:
       #ifdef __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__
           scriptManager.Update(deltaTime);
       #endif
   - This evaluates all DETECT_COLLISION rules registered by the active script
     on every rendered frame, using the same clamped delta time as all other
     per-frame systems.

Usage:
  To enable ScriptManager:   uncomment  #define __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__  in Includes.h
  To disable ScriptManager:  re-comment #define __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__  in Includes.h
