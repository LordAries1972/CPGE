main.cpp - Changes 27-04-2026
==============================

Added OSD mutual-exclusion logic:

1. New atomic flag bDismissAllSettingOSDs (near line 207) — set by the F2
   key handler in KBHandlersCode.cpp; consumed once per frame in the OSD
   processing section.

2. Two lambdas defined just before the MIC volume OSD block:
   - dismissAllVolOSDs()       — removes all five volume OSD windows.
     Called when bDismissAllSettingOSDs fires (F2 pressed).
   - dismissOtherVolOSDs(id)   — closes every OTHER volume OSD window AND
     clears renderer->bDebugOSDActive. Called at the top of each volume
     OSD activation block so a new setting OSD always replaces the old one.

3. Each of the five volume OSD blocks (MIC, MUSIC, SFX, MASTER, TTS) now
   calls dismissOtherVolOSDs(CURRENT_OSD_ID) as its first action when
   adj != 0, before creating or updating its own window.
