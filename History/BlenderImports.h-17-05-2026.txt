BlenderImports.h — History 17-05-2026
Rewritten with per-renderer coordinate system handling and conditional types.

Changes:
- Added detailed coordinate system documentation block:
  GLTF native: right-handed Y-up (+Y up, +Z toward viewer)
  DirectX: left-handed Y-up (flip Z: -Z forward)
  OpenGL: same handedness as GLTF (no flip needed)
  Vulkan: Y-down (flip Y from GLTF)
- GLTF_DEFAULT_FLIP constexpr now renderer-conditional:
  DX11/DX12: FLIP_Z
  Vulkan: FLIP_Y
  OpenGL/default: FLIP_NONE
- ConvertNodeMatrix signature conditional:
  DX11/DX12: static XMMATRIX ConvertNodeMatrix(const XMMATRIX& m, AxisFlipFlags f) noexcept;
  OpenGL/Vulkan: static Matrix4x4 ConvertNodeMatrix(const Matrix4x4& m, AxisFlipFlags f) noexcept;
- BuildConfig uses GLTF_DEFAULT_FLIP instead of hardcoded FLIP_Z.
