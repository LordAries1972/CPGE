FBXAnimator.h - Created 23 June 2026
=====================================
NEW FILE: Native FBX animation playback header, parallel to GLTFAnimator.h.

Structures added:
  FBXAnimChannel  -- one animated property (Translation/Rotation/Scale) on one FBX model node.
                     Contains resolved FBXAnimCurve copies for X/Y/Z axes, plus preRotation
                     degrees and rotationOrder needed for correct Euler->Quat conversion.
  FBXAnimationClip -- one FBX AnimStack in engine-friendly form (name, startTime, duration,
                      vector of FBXAnimChannel).

Class FBXAnimator:
  Public interface mirrors GLTFAnimator exactly so ModelAnimator can dispatch transparently.
  Parse:   ParseAnimationsFromFBX(FBXScene, fbxIDToModelSlot)
  Control: CreateAnimationInstance, StartAnimation, StopAnimation, PauseAnimation,
           ResumeAnimation, UpdateAnimations, ForceAnimationReset
  Query:   GetAnimationCount, GetClip, GetAnimationInstance, IsAnimationPlaying,
           GetAnimationTime, GetAnimationDuration
  Params:  SetAnimationSpeed, SetAnimationLooping, SetAnimationTime, SetAnimationDirection,
           AtAnimationEndFrame, HoldAnimationAtFrame
  Cleanup: ClearAllAnimations, RemoveAnimationInstance

Private helpers:
  EvaluateCurve, EvaluateCurveOrDefault, GetInterpolationTypeForKey
  ApplyCoordFlip (Z-negate for RH->LH), ApplyRotationFlip (M*R*M similarity transform)
  EulerToMatrix (all 6 rotation orders), MatrixToQuaternion (DirectX row-vector Shepperd)
  SlerpQuaternions, ApplyChannelToSlot
  IsInHierarchy, ResetLocalTRSToBase, RecomposeWorldFromLocalTRS
  LogInfo, LogWarning, LogError
