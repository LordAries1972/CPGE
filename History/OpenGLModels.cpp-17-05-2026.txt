OpenGLModels.cpp — History 17-05-2026
NEW FILE — OpenGL model utility implementation.

Purpose: Implements OpenGL VAO/VBO/EBO vertex buffer management, texture loading, and shader utilities.

Contents:
- stb_image texture loading (conditional on __has_include)
- Vertex attrib layout: stride=44 bytes
    location 0: position (vec3, 12 bytes)
    location 1: normal   (vec3, 12 bytes)
    location 2: texCoord (vec2,  8 bytes)
    location 3: tangent  (vec3, 12 bytes)
- glGenVertexArrays / glGenBuffers / GL_ARRAY_BUFFER / GL_ELEMENT_ARRAY_BUFFER / GL_STATIC_DRAW
- glGetUniformLocation-based uniform setting for material and transform UBOs
- OpenGLModelBuffers::Upload(): creates VAO+VBO+EBO from vertex/index data
- OpenGLModelBuffers::Destroy(): deletes all GL handles
- Guard: #if defined(__USE_OPENGL__)
