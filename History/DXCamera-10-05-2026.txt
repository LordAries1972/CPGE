==========================

SetupDefaultCamera() — sync position/target public members (line ~2568)

Added two lines after XMStoreFloat3 extractions so that GetPosition() and the
target member stay consistent after a default-camera reset:

    position = eyePos;
    target   = lookPos;

Previously SetupDefaultCamera() only updated viewMatrix (via SetViewMatrix) and
projectionMatrix. After the tunnel fly-through moved position/target via
SetPosition()/SetTarget(), calling SetupDefaultCamera() left the public members
stale, causing the next frame's GetPosition() call to return the tunnel position
instead of the default camera position.
