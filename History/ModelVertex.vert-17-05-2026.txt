ModelVertex.vert — History 17-05-2026
NEW FILE — Vulkan vertex shader (GLSL 450 / SPIR-V source).

Purpose: Vulkan vertex shader, mirrors ModelVertex.glsl (OpenGL) with Vulkan clip-space conventions.
         Compiled to SPIR-V via glslangValidator or shaderc at build time.

Shader: GLSL 450 (Vulkan)
UBO: layout(set=0, binding=0) uniform ConstantBuffer (world/view/proj/camera/scale)
Vertex inputs: location 0-3 (position/normal/texCoord/tangent)
Outputs: vWorldPosition, vNormal, vTexCoord, vViewDirection, vTangent, vBitangent
Helper: inverse3x3() — same shear-safe normal matrix approach as OpenGL version
Vulkan clip-space fix:
  clipPos.y = -clipPos.y  (flip Y: Vulkan NDC is Y-down)
  clipPos.z = (clipPos.z + clipPos.w) * 0.5  (remap Z from [-1,1] to [0,1])
