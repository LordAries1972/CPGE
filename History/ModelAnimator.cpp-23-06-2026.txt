ModelAnimator.cpp - Created 23 June 2026
=========================================
NEW FILE: Implementation of universal animation dispatcher.

Dispatch logic:
  IsAnimationPlaying  -- OR of both sub-animator results
  StartAnimation      -- switch on importType: GLTF->gltfAnimator, FBX->fbxAnimator,
                         NONE/unknown: tries gltfAnimator first, then fbxAnimator
  Stop/Pause/Resume   -- call both, return (a || b)
  UpdateAnimations    -- calls gltfAnimator.UpdateAnimations(dt, m_models, m_maxModels) then
                         fbxAnimator.UpdateAnimations(dt, m_models, m_maxModels)
  GetAnimationTime    -- prefers playing instance; falls back to whichever has non-zero time
  SetAnimationDirection, HoldAnimationAtFrame -- applied to both
  ClearAllAnimations  -- clears both

Rationale: calling both sub-animators for Stop/Pause/etc. is safe because each animator
           ignores parentModelIDs it doesn't own, making no-ops harmless.
