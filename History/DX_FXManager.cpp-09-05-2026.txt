   - Now acquires m_effectsMutex to prevent render-thread race.

Thread safety fix: RENDERER_IS_THREAD means the render thread runs independently
and iterates the effects vector concurrently. SaveAndSuspendFXForScene and
RestoreFXAfterScene are called from the main thread. Both now hold m_effectsMutex
for their entire body. StopWarpDotTunnel/StopStarfield/SafelyClearAllEffects do
NOT acquire m_effectsMutex themselves, so no deadlock.
