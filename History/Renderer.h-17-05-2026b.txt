Renderer.h — History 17-05-2026b
Fixed XMFLOAT4 guard; conditional camera header include.

Changes:
- Blit2DColoredPixel guard split: was '#if defined(_WIN64) || defined(_WIN32)' (too broad — XMFLOAT4
  undefined on OpenGL builds). Changed to:
  #if defined(__USE_DIRECTX_11__) || defined(__USE_DIRECTX_12__)
      virtual void Blit2DColoredPixel(int x, int y, float pixelSize, XMFLOAT4 color) = 0;
  #elif defined(__USE_OPENGL__) || defined(__USE_VULKAN__)
      virtual void Blit2DColoredPixel(int x, int y, float pixelSize, const Vector4& color) = 0;
  #endif
- Blit2DWrappedObjectAtOffset remains under '#if defined(_WIN64) || defined(_WIN32)' (no DX types).
- Camera header include made conditional:
  #if defined(__USE_DIRECTX_11__) || defined(__USE_DIRECTX_12__) -> DXCamera.h
  #elif defined(__USE_OPENGL__) -> OpenGLCamera.h
  #elif defined(__USE_VULKAN__) -> VulkanCamera.h
  (Previously hardcoded DXCamera.h; caused C3646 unknown-type on OpenGL builds)
