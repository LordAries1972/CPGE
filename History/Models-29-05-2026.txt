FIX 2: SetEnvironmentProperties envTint field-wise assignment.
Models.cpp SetEnvironmentProperties used "m_modelInfo.envTint = tint" where
envTint is Vector3 but tint is XMFLOAT3 (DirectX::XMFLOAT3 on Windows+Vulkan).
No implicit conversion operator exists. Fixed by field-wise copy:
    m_modelInfo.envTint.x = tint.x;
    m_modelInfo.envTint.y = tint.y;
    m_modelInfo.envTint.z = tint.z;
Works for all builds since both Vector3 and XMFLOAT3 expose .x/.y/.z float fields.
