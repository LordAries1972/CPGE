Changes Made (2026-05-11):
--------------------------
Integrated ScriptManager into main.cpp with full #ifdef __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__
conditional compilation guards so the entire integration is zero-cost when the
define is commented out in Includes.h.

1. Include block (after #include "ScreenRecorder.h"):
   - Added:
       #ifdef __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__
       #include "ScriptManager.h"
       #endif

2. Global declarations (after ScreenRecorder screenRecorder):
   - Added:
       #ifdef __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__
       ScriptManager scriptManager;
       #endif

3. Startup initialisation (after scene.Initialize(renderer)):
   - Added:
       scriptManager.Initialize(&fxManager, &threadManager, &soundManager,
                                &guiManager, &scene, &gamePlayer, nullptr,
                                &js, &renderer->myCamera);
       scriptManager.LoadSceneScript(SceneType::SCENE_INITIALISE);
       scriptManager.ExecuteScriptAsync();
   - nullptr is passed for Physics* because Physics is not instantiated in
     main.cpp; ScriptManager safely guards against null before any physics call.

4. SwitchToGamePlay():
   - Before scene.CleanUp(): scriptManager.StopExecution()
   - After scene.InitiateScene(): scriptManager.LoadSceneScript(SCENE_GAMEPLAY)
                                  scriptManager.ExecuteScriptAsync()

5. SwitchToMovieIntro():
   - Before scene.SetGotoScene(SCENE_INTRO_MOVIE): scriptManager.StopExecution()
   - After scene.InitiateScene(): scriptManager.LoadSceneScript(SCENE_INTRO_MOVIE)
                                  scriptManager.ExecuteScriptAsync()

6. SwitchToGameIntro():
   - Before scene.CleanUp(): scriptManager.StopExecution()
   - After scene.InitiateScene(): scriptManager.LoadSceneScript(SCENE_GAMETITLE)
                                  scriptManager.ExecuteScriptAsync()

Usage:
  To enable ScriptManager:   uncomment  #define __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__  in Includes.h
  To disable ScriptManager:  re-comment #define __USE_SCRIPT_MANAGER__  in Includes.h
