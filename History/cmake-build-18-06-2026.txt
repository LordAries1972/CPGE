cmake-build.bat - 18 June 2026
-------------------------------
Extended the version-increment PowerShell one-liner (SKIP_VERSION_INCREMENT block) to also
update the *Current Build Version: vX.Y.Z* line at the top of ReleaseInfo.md.

Previously the block wrote only Version.id and BuildInfo.h; the header line in ReleaseInfo.md
drifted behind after every build. Now all three files are kept in sync atomically within the
same PowerShell invocation:
  1. Version.id                  - unchanged behaviour
  2. BuildInfo.h                 - unchanged behaviour
  3. ReleaseInfo.md line 6       - regex replaces *Current Build Version: vOLD* with vNEW*

The SKIP_VERSION_INCREMENT guard is unchanged, so "cmake-build all" still increments exactly
once (first pipeline only) and all three files are updated together on that single increment.
