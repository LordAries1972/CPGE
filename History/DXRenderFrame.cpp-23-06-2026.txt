DXRenderFrame.cpp - Modified 23 June 2026
==========================================
Replaced hard-coded GLTFAnimator calls with unified ModelAnimator calls:
  scene.gltfAnimator.IsAnimationPlaying(iModelID)
    -> scene.modelAnimator.IsAnimationPlaying(iModelID)
  scene.gltfAnimator.UpdateAnimations(deltaTime, scene.scene_models, MAX_MODELS)
    -> scene.modelAnimator.UpdateAnimations(deltaTime)

Affected locations: SCENE_GAMETITLE and SCENE_GAMEPLAY blocks (2 occurrences each).
The new calls handle both GLTF and FBX animated models automatically.
