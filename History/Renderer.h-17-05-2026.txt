17-05-2026 - Renderer.h

CHANGE: Replaced unconditional #include "DXCamera.h" with a renderer-conditional include block.

Previously:
    #include "DXCamera.h"

Now:
    #if defined(__USE_DIRECTX_11__) || defined(__USE_DIRECTX_12__)
        #include "DXCamera.h"
    #elif defined(__USE_OPENGL__)
        #include "OpenGLCamera.h"
    #elif defined(__USE_VULKAN__)
        #include "VulkanCamera.h"
    #endif

WHY: DXCamera.h wraps the Camera class in #if defined(__USE_DIRECTX_11__) || defined(__USE_DIRECTX_12__).
When only __USE_OPENGL__ is active, Camera is never defined, causing:
    error C3646: 'myCamera': unknown override specifier  (line 276)
The fix ensures the correct camera header is included based on which renderer define is active.
