MoviePlayer.h - 06-05-2026
A/V sync fix: audio advancing ahead of video

Changes:
- Added m_samplesPlayedOffset (UINT64): accumulates SamplesPlayed across pause/resume cycles
  because XAudio2 resets SamplesPlayed to 0 on every Start() of a stopped voice.
- Added m_firstAudioSamplePTS (LONGLONG, -1 = not anchored): stores the MF file PTS of
  the first audio buffer submitted to XAudio2, so the SamplesPlayed counter can be mapped
  to the file's actual PTS timeline rather than "time since Play() was called."
- Updated comment on m_audioReadPosition to reflect its new role as the read-ahead gate.
